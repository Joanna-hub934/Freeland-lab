Molecular Ecology Lab

Trent University

We investigate the interactions among evolution, ecology, and environment in natural plant populations

We use a range of different tools including genetic analyses, and field and greenhouse experiments, to investigate how processes including reproduction (sexual and clonal), dispersal, local adaptation, and competition interact to inform the biodiversity of plant communities across different types of wetland habitats.
